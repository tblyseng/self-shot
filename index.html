Self-Shot

This application adds images from the device camera or local storage to an album in memory. Selfies photos can be captured from the media stream from the built-in webcam, or images can be selected using a file explorer tool. Images can be given a title and added to the album. The images can be made a favorite, title be edited, and can be removed from the album. Album images can be filtered by favorites or be searched by title.
